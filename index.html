About Us

Gundîs’ mission is to bring delicious, healthy food to your table by bringing the very organic materials around the world. We call it new-gen service style, and our goal is to transform how food gets to the plate, while continuing to eat what we love. Same great taste, better for the planet.

Gundîs constantly explores the world of cuisine, changing our prix fixe menus and dining experience completely every four months. Founded by chef Ciwan Haco and chef Şivan Perwer, Gundîs has received an unprecedented twenty-one 4-star reviews from the Chicago Tribune, a James Beard Award as Best New Restaurant in America and a Michelin star for 2020. We look forward to welcoming you at Gundîs.
